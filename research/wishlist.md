- Pickling cucumbers
- Bell peppers
- Hot peppers (jalapeño, serrano, etc.)

- Leaf lettuce (cut-and-come-again)
- Romaine lettuce
- Butterhead lettuce (Boston/Bibb)
- Arugula (self-seeds)
- Curly kale
- Collard greens (1 plant only)

## Root Vegetables (6)
- Carrots
- Beets (roots + greens)
- Radishes (30 days)
- Turnips (roots + greens)
- Parsnips (long season, sweeter after frost)
- Potatoes (great for BTE method)

## Legumes (3)
- Bush beans
- Snap peas
- Edamame

## Alliums (4)
- Garlic - hardneck (plant in fall)
- Yellow onions
- Scallions
- Shallots

## Brassicas (3)
- Cauliflower
- Cabbage (green)
- Broccoli raab

## Herbs (16)
### Annuals (replant yearly)
- Basil
- Cilantro (bolts in heat)
- Flat-leaf parsley
- Curly parsley
- Dill (self-seeds)
- Chamomile (self-seeds)
- Stevia

### Perennials (permanent location)
- Oregano
- Thyme
- Rosemary (marginal in 6b, may need winter protection)
- Sage
- Tarragon
- Lavender (needs good drainage)

### Spreaders (MUST CONTAIN in pots)
- Mint
- Lemon balm

### Isolate (inhibits other plants)
- Fennel

## Perennial Vegetables (3)
- Asparagus (2-3 years to establish, then 15-25 years production)
- Rhubarb (decades of production)
- Horseradish (CONTAIN - spreads aggressively)

## Berries & Fruits (2)
- Blueberries (need acidic soil amendment)
- Grapes (need trellis/arbor structure)
