## Path Design

All paths: **2ft wide**, mulched with wood chips from your log pile

Benefits:
- Wheelbarrow accessible (standard is 24-30" wide)
- Comfortable walking
- Suppresses weeds
- Free material (your chipped logs)

## Water Access

### Back to Eden Watering Schedule

| Year | Frequency | Notes |
|------|-----------|-------|
| Year 1 | Regular | Establish transplants, new seeds |
| Year 2 | Reduced | Mostly dry spells only |
| Year 3+ | Minimal | Some BTE gardeners water only a few times per year |

### Setup Options

**Simple:** Soaker hose under mulch (~$50-75)
- Turn on manually during dry spells
- Probably sufficient for BTE

**Automated:** Drip with timer (~$150-300)
- Set and forget for Year 1
- May become unnecessary

## Planting Calendar (Zone 6b)

| Timing | Crops |
|--------|-------|
| **March (indoors)** | Start tomatoes, peppers, eggplant |
| **April (indoors)** | Start squash, cucumbers |
| **Late April** | Direct sow peas, plant potatoes |
| **May 1-15** | Direct sow greens, beets, carrots |
| **After May 15** | Transplant warm crops (frost-free) |
| **June** | Succession plant beans, greens |
| **July** | Plant fall brassicas |
| **October** | Plant garlic for next year |
