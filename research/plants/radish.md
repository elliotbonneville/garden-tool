# Radishes

## Overview
Radishes are the fastest vegetable to harvest - ready in just 3-4 weeks! Perfect for impatient gardeners and succession planting.

## Growing Requirements

| Requirement | Details |
|-------------|---------|
| Sun | Full sun to partial shade |
| Soil pH | 6.0-7.0 |
| Spacing | 1-2 inches apart |
| Days to Harvest | 22-30 days |
| Water | 1 inch per week |

## Planting Timeline (Zone 6b)
- **Spring Sow**: March 15 - May 15
- **Fall Sow**: August 15 - September 30
- **Avoid**: Hot summer months

## Care Tips
1. **Fast & Easy** - Great beginner vegetable
2. **Succession Plant** - Small sowings every week
3. **Harvest Promptly** - Become pithy if left too long
4. **Interplant** - Mark slow-germinating seeds (carrots)

## Common Issues
- **Pithiness** - Harvest before they get too large
- **All Tops, No Roots** - Too much nitrogen, too much shade
- **Flea Beetles** - Row cover, or just ignore (cosmetic damage)

## Companion Plants
Good companions: Carrots, lettuce, peas, spinach
Avoid planting near: Hyssop

## Expected Yield
1 radish per inch of row
