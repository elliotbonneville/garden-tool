# Beets

## Overview
Beets are a two-for-one vegetable - both roots and greens are edible. Fast-growing and tolerant of cool weather.

## Growing Requirements

| Requirement | Details |
|-------------|---------|
| Sun | Full sun to partial shade |
| Soil pH | 6.0-7.5 |
| Spacing | 3-4 inches apart |
| Days to Harvest | 50-70 days |
| Water | 1 inch per week |

## Planting Timeline (Zone 6b)
- **Spring Sow**: April 1 - May 30
- **Fall Sow**: July 15 - August 15
- **Note**: Pull back mulch for direct seeding

## Care Tips
1. **Thin Early** - Each "seed" is actually a cluster
2. **Eat the Greens** - As nutritious as the roots
3. **Harvest Young** - 1.5-2 inch roots are most tender
4. **Succession Plant** - Every 3 weeks

## Common Issues
- **Woody Roots** - Harvest before too large
- **Leaf Miners** - Row cover, remove affected leaves
- **Poor Germination** - Soak seeds overnight before planting

## Companion Plants
Good companions: Onions, lettuce, cabbage family, bush beans
Avoid planting near: Pole beans

## Expected Yield
1 lb per foot of row
