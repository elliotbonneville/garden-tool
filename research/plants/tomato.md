# Tomatoes

## Overview
Tomatoes are one of the most rewarding garden crops, producing abundant harvests throughout the summer. They thrive in Zone 6b with proper care.

## Varieties for This Garden
- **Cherry Tomatoes** - Prolific producers, great for snacking
- **Slicing Tomatoes** - Classic sandwich tomatoes

## Growing Requirements

| Requirement | Details |
|-------------|---------|
| Sun | Full sun (8+ hours) |
| Soil pH | 6.0-6.8 |
| Spacing | 24-36 inches apart |
| Days to Harvest | 60-85 days |
| Water | 1-2 inches per week |

## Planting Timeline (Zone 6b)
- **Start Indoors**: March 15 - April 1
- **Transplant Outdoors**: May 15-25 (after last frost)
- **First Harvest**: July - August

## Care Tips
1. **Staking/Caging** - Support plants early to prevent breakage
2. **Pruning** - Remove suckers for indeterminate varieties
3. **Mulching** - Helps retain moisture and prevent soil-borne diseases
4. **Consistent Watering** - Prevents blossom end rot

## Common Issues
- **Blossom End Rot** - Caused by calcium deficiency/inconsistent watering
- **Early Blight** - Fungal disease, remove affected leaves
- **Hornworms** - Hand-pick or use BT spray

## Companion Plants
Good companions: Basil, carrots, parsley, marigolds
Avoid planting near: Brassicas, fennel

## Expected Yield
- Cherry: 10-20 lbs per plant
- Slicing: 10-15 lbs per plant
