# Watermelon

## Overview
Watermelon needs heat and space but rewards with sweet summer fruit. Choose short-season varieties for Zone 6b success.

## Varieties for Zone 6b
- **Sugar Baby** - Small (8-10 lb), early, space-efficient
- **Crimson Sweet** - Classic flavor, 15-25 lbs
- **Yellow Doll** - Yellow flesh, early

## Growing Requirements

| Requirement | Details |
|-------------|---------|
| Sun | Full sun (8+ hours) |
| Soil pH | 6.0-6.8 |
| Spacing | 6-8 feet apart |
| Days to Harvest | 70-90 days |
| Water | 1-2 inches per week, reduce near harvest |

## Care Tips
1. **Warm soil** - Use black plastic mulch
2. **Hill planting** - Improves drainage and warmth
3. **Reduce water at ripening** - Concentrates sugars
4. **Check ripeness** - Yellow ground spot, hollow thump

## Common Issues
- **Poor fruit set** - Hand pollinate if needed
- **Anthracnose** - Fungal spots, avoid overhead water
- **Not sweet** - Needs consistent heat and reduced water at end

## Companion Plants
Good companions: Corn, radishes, nasturtiums
Avoid planting near: Potatoes

## Expected Yield
- 2-4 melons per plant
