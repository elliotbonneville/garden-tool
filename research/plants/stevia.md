# Stevia

## Overview
Stevia is a natural sweetener plant - leaves are 30-40x sweeter than sugar with zero calories. Grow as an annual in Zone 6b.

## Varieties for This Garden
- **Stevia rebaudiana** - Standard variety
- **Sugar Leaf** - Selected for sweetness

## Growing Requirements

| Requirement | Details |
|-------------|---------|
| Sun | Full sun to part shade |
| Soil pH | 6.5-7.0 |
| Spacing | 12-18 inches apart |
| Days to Harvest | 90-120 days |
| Water | Consistent moisture |

## Planting Timeline (Zone 6b)
- **Start Indoors**: March 1-15 (slow to start)
- **Transplant**: After last frost, May 20+
- **Harvest**: Summer through fall

## Care Tips
1. **Buy plants** - Seeds are difficult to germinate
2. **Pinch for bushiness** - Prevents legginess
3. **Harvest before flowering** - Sweetest leaves
4. **Dry for storage** - Crush dried leaves for sweetener

## Common Issues
- **Difficult from seed** - Buy transplants instead
- **Frost sensitive** - Treat as annual
- **Leggy growth** - Pinch regularly

## Companion Plants
Good companions: Most vegetables and herbs
Avoid planting near: Nothing specific

## Expected Yield
- Fresh leaves all season, dry for year-round use
