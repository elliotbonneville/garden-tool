# Eggplant

## Overview
Eggplant is a heat-loving crop that produces beautiful purple fruits. Requires similar conditions to peppers and benefits from the long growing season.

## Growing Requirements

| Requirement | Details |
|-------------|---------|
| Sun | Full sun (8+ hours) |
| Soil pH | 6.0-6.5 |
| Spacing | 24-30 inches apart |
| Days to Harvest | 70-85 days |
| Water | 1-2 inches per week |

## Planting Timeline (Zone 6b)
- **Start Indoors**: February 15 - March 1
- **Transplant Outdoors**: May 25 - June 5 (soil temp 70Â°F+)

## Care Tips
1. **Heat Requirements** - Needs warm soil and air temperatures
2. **Staking** - Support heavy fruits to prevent breakage
3. **Harvest Young** - Pick when skin is glossy, not dull
4. **Mulch Heavily** - Retains warmth and moisture

## Common Issues
- **Flea Beetles** - Row cover early in season
- **Verticillium Wilt** - Rotate crops, choose resistant varieties
- **Poor Fruit Set** - Usually temperature related

## Companion Plants
Good companions: Peppers, tomatoes, beans, marigolds
Avoid planting near: Fennel

## Expected Yield
4-6 fruits per plant
