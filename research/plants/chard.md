# Swiss Chard

## Overview
Swiss chard is a beautiful, productive green that tolerates both heat and cold. Rainbow varieties add color to the garden and table.

## Varieties for This Garden
- **Rainbow Chard** - Colorful stems (red, yellow, orange, white)

## Growing Requirements

| Requirement | Details |
|-------------|---------|
| Sun | Full sun to partial shade |
| Soil pH | 6.0-7.0 |
| Spacing | 8-12 inches apart |
| Days to Harvest | 50-60 days |
| Water | 1-1.5 inches per week |

## Planting Timeline (Zone 6b)
- **Spring Direct Sow**: April 15 - May 30
- **Fall Sow**: July 15 - August 15
- **Harvest**: All season long

## Care Tips
1. **Heat Tolerant** - Unlike spinach, handles summer
2. **Cut-and-Come-Again** - Harvest outer leaves regularly
3. **Colorful Stems** - Leave some for garden beauty
4. **Light Frost OK** - Can extend into fall

## Common Issues
- **Leaf Miners** - Row cover, remove affected leaves
- **Cercospora Leaf Spot** - Remove infected leaves, improve air flow
- **Slugs** - Beer traps, diatomaceous earth

## Companion Plants
Good companions: Beans, cabbage, onions, lettuce
Avoid planting near: Corn, potatoes

## Expected Yield
1-2 lbs per plant over the season
