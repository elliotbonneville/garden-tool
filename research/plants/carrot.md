# Carrots

## Overview
Carrots are a rewarding root vegetable that taste far superior when homegrown. Require loose soil and patience for germination.

## Growing Requirements

| Requirement | Details |
|-------------|---------|
| Sun | Full sun to partial shade |
| Soil pH | 6.0-6.8 |
| Spacing | 2-3 inches apart |
| Days to Harvest | 70-80 days |
| Water | 1 inch per week |

## Planting Timeline (Zone 6b)
- **Spring Sow**: April 1 - May 15
- **Fall Sow**: July 1 - August 1
- **Note**: Pull back mulch for direct seeding

## Care Tips
1. **Loose Soil** - Essential for straight roots
2. **Thin Ruthlessly** - Crowded carrots fork
3. **Keep Moist** - Until germination (can take 2-3 weeks!)
4. **Patience** - Slow to germinate, then grows steadily

## Common Issues
- **Forked Roots** - Rocks, compacted soil, or not thinned
- **Carrot Rust Fly** - Row cover, interplant with onions
- **Splitting** - Inconsistent watering

## Companion Plants
Good companions: Onions, leeks, rosemary, lettuce, tomatoes
Avoid planting near: Dill

## Expected Yield
1 lb per foot of row
