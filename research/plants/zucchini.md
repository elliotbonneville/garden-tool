# Zucchini & Summer Squash

## Overview
Zucchini is one of the most productive garden vegetables. A few plants can feed a family all summer. Part of the summer squash family.

## Varieties for This Garden
- **Zucchini** - Classic green summer squash
- **Yellow Squash** - Milder flavor, same growing requirements

## Growing Requirements

| Requirement | Details |
|-------------|---------|
| Sun | Full sun (6+ hours) |
| Soil pH | 6.0-7.5 |
| Spacing | 24-36 inches apart |
| Days to Harvest | 45-55 days |
| Water | 1-2 inches per week |

## Planting Timeline (Zone 6b)
- **Direct Sow**: May 15-30
- **First Harvest**: Late June - July

## Care Tips
1. **Don't Overplant** - 2-3 plants per family is plenty!
2. **Harvest Small** - 6-8 inches for best flavor/texture
3. **Check Daily** - Fruits grow FAST
4. **Pollination** - Hand pollinate if needed

## Common Issues
- **Squash Vine Borers** - Cover stems with foil, succession plant
- **Powdery Mildew** - Improve air circulation, water at base
- **Poor Pollination** - Hand pollinate in early morning

## Companion Plants
Good companions: Corn, beans, radishes, marigolds
Avoid planting near: Potatoes

## Expected Yield
6-10 lbs per plant (can be MUCH more!)
