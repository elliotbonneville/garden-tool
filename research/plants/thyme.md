# Thyme

## Overview
Thyme is a versatile culinary herb that's also ornamental. Hardy perennial that tolerates poor soil and drought.

## Growing Requirements

| Requirement | Details |
|-------------|---------|
| Sun | Full sun |
| Soil pH | 6.0-8.0 |
| Spacing | 6-12 inches apart |
| Days to Harvest | 6-8 weeks from transplant |
| Water | Drought tolerant |

## Planting Timeline (Zone 6b)
- **Transplant**: After last frost (May)
- **Harvest**: All season long
- **Overwinter**: Hardy perennial

## Care Tips
1. **Well-Drained Soil** - Essential, hates wet feet
2. **Trim After Flowering** - Keeps plant compact
3. **Harvest Anytime** - Fresh or dried
4. **Replace Every 3-4 Years** - Plants get woody

## Common Issues
- **Root Rot** - Too much moisture
- **Winter Die-Back** - Ensure good drainage
- **Fungal Issues** - Improve air circulation

## Companion Plants
Good companions: Cabbage, tomatoes, eggplant, strawberries
Avoid planting near: None significant

## Expected Yield
Multiple harvests per season
