# Chives

## Overview
Chives are the easiest herb to grow - a hardy perennial with mild onion flavor. Pretty purple flowers are also edible.

## Growing Requirements

| Requirement | Details |
|-------------|---------|
| Sun | Full sun to partial shade |
| Soil pH | 6.0-7.0 |
| Spacing | 6-8 inches apart |
| Days to Harvest | 60 days from seed, immediate from transplant |
| Water | 1 inch per week |

## Planting Timeline (Zone 6b)
- **Transplant**: Early spring or fall
- **Harvest**: All season long
- **Overwinter**: Very hardy perennial

## Care Tips
1. **Cut Often** - Promotes fresh growth
2. **Divide Every 3 Years** - Keeps plants vigorous
3. **Edible Flowers** - Harvest when fully open
4. **Let Some Flower** - Attracts pollinators

## Common Issues
- **Virtually Problem-Free** - One of the easiest herbs
- **Floppy Growth** - Cut back to encourage upright stems
- **Self-Seeding** - Remove flowers if unwanted spread

## Companion Plants
Good companions: Carrots, tomatoes, roses
Avoid planting near: Beans, peas

## Expected Yield
Continuous harvest all season
