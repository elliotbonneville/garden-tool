# Cauliflower

## Overview
Cauliflower is the most finicky brassica, requiring consistent conditions. Worth growing for fresh-from-garden taste but expect some failures.

## Varieties for This Garden
- **Snow Crown** - Reliable, early
- **Amazing** - Heat tolerant
- **Cheddar** - Orange color, extra nutrition

## Growing Requirements

| Requirement | Details |
|-------------|---------|
| Sun | Full sun |
| Soil pH | 6.0-7.0 |
| Spacing | 18-24 inches apart |
| Days to Harvest | 55-80 days |
| Water | 1-2 inches per week, consistent |

## Planting Timeline (Zone 6b)
- **Spring Start Indoors**: March 1-15
- **Transplant**: April 15-May 1
- **Fall Crop** (more reliable): Start July 1, transplant Aug 1
- **Harvest**: June or October-November

## Care Tips
1. **Blanch white varieties** - Tie leaves over head when 2-3"
2. **Steady conditions** - Temperature swings cause buttoning
3. **Fall crop easier** - More consistent cool weather
4. **Rich soil** - Heavy feeder

## Common Issues
- **Buttoning** - Small heads from stress
- **Browning curds** - Sun exposure, blanch earlier
- **Cabbage worms** - Use BT or row covers
- **Bolting** - From heat or stress

## Companion Plants
Good companions: Beans, celery, onions
Avoid planting near: Strawberries, tomatoes

## Expected Yield
- 1 head per plant (1-2 lbs)
