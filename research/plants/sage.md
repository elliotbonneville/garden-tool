# Sage

## Overview
Sage is a beautiful, aromatic perennial herb. Essential for stuffing and pairs well with rich meats. Attractive gray-green foliage.

## Growing Requirements

| Requirement | Details |
|-------------|---------|
| Sun | Full sun |
| Soil pH | 6.0-7.0 |
| Spacing | 18-24 inches apart |
| Days to Harvest | 75 days from seed |
| Water | Drought tolerant once established |

## Planting Timeline (Zone 6b)
- **Transplant**: After last frost (May)
- **Harvest**: All season, best before flowering
- **Overwinter**: Hardy with some protection

## Care Tips
1. **Don't Overwater** - Prone to root rot
2. **Prune Annually** - In spring, prevents woodiness
3. **Harvest Lightly First Year** - Let plant establish
4. **Replace Every 4-5 Years** - Plants get woody

## Common Issues
- **Woody Stems** - Normal with age, prune or replace
- **Root Rot** - Improve drainage
- **Powdery Mildew** - Improve air circulation

## Companion Plants
Good companions: Rosemary, cabbage, carrots, tomatoes
Avoid planting near: Cucumbers

## Expected Yield
Multiple harvests per season, dries excellently
