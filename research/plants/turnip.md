# Turnips

## Overview
Turnips are fast-growing root vegetables. Both roots and greens are edible. They thrive in cool weather and mature quickly.

## Varieties for This Garden
- **Purple Top White Globe** - Classic variety
- **Hakurei** - Japanese, sweet and tender
- **Seven Top** - Grown primarily for greens

## Growing Requirements

| Requirement | Details |
|-------------|---------|
| Sun | Full sun to part shade |
| Soil pH | 6.0-7.5 |
| Spacing | 4-6 inches apart |
| Days to Harvest | 30-60 days |
| Water | 1 inch per week |

## Planting Timeline (Zone 6b)
- **Spring Sowing**: March 15-April 15
- **Fall Sowing**: August 1-September 1
- **Harvest**: Ongoing, frost improves flavor

## Care Tips
1. **Direct sow** - Doesn't transplant well
2. **Thin early** - Crowding causes small roots
3. **Harvest young** - 2-3" diameter is ideal
4. **Fall crop sweeter** - Frost converts starch to sugar

## Common Issues
- **Woody roots** - Harvest younger, keep watered
- **Flea beetles** - Row covers, they attack young plants
- **Root maggots** - Rotate crops, use collars

## Companion Plants
Good companions: Peas, beans
Avoid planting near: Other brassicas (rotation)

## Expected Yield
- 3-5 lbs per 10-foot row
