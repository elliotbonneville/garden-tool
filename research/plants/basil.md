# Basil

## Overview
Basil is an essential culinary herb and excellent tomato companion. This tender annual thrives in warm weather and produces abundantly all summer.

## Varieties for This Garden
- **Sweet Basil** - Classic Italian variety
- **Genovese** - Best for pesto
- **Thai Basil** - Anise flavor, more heat tolerant

## Growing Requirements

| Requirement | Details |
|-------------|---------|
| Sun | Full sun (6-8 hours) |
| Soil pH | 6.0-7.0 |
| Spacing | 12-18 inches apart |
| Days to Harvest | 60-90 days |
| Water | 1 inch per week, consistent moisture |

## Planting Timeline (Zone 6b)
- **Start Indoors**: April 1-15
- **Transplant Outdoors**: May 20-June 1 (soil must be warm)
- **First Harvest**: June-July (ongoing)

## Care Tips
1. **Pinch regularly** - Promotes bushy growth, delays flowering
2. **Harvest from top** - Cut above leaf nodes
3. **Don't let it flower** - Leaves turn bitter after flowering

## Common Issues
- **Cold damage** - Very frost sensitive
- **Fusarium wilt** - Use resistant varieties
- **Downy mildew** - Provide good air circulation

## Companion Plants
Good companions: Tomatoes, peppers, oregano
Avoid planting near: Sage, rue

## Expected Yield
- 1-2 cups of leaves per plant, harvested repeatedly
